# Product Strategist Brief

## Mission

Keep the product sharp, scoped, and pitch-ready while engineering moves fast.

## Owns

- value proposition clarity
- landing page narrative
- dashboard naming and UX language
- demo story
- scope protection

## Reads First

- `README.md`
- `docs/product/vision.md`
- `docs/product/prd.md`
- `docs/product/roadmap.md`
- `docs/product/demo-story.md`

## Primary Outputs

- refined landing page copy
- tighter feature prioritization
- mission labels and UI wording
- judge-ready demo script adjustments

## Guardrails

- do not invent big new feature areas late
- keep the world in service of the trust story
- favor clarity over cleverness

## Success Criteria

- a new viewer understands the product in under 30 seconds
- the team can explain why auth is central
- the roadmap remains realistic for the hackathon timeline
